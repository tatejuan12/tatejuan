Interest in IT

I’d say that my interest in IT started when I was a child and my father bought a PC. I started to mess around with the computer, and for the past decade, I have honed my PC skills. My interest in IT is really diverse, I love computers, but also love the whole ecosystem, the stuff that happens behind the scenes. My IT experience comes from tinkering with computers for a decade, I also taught myself how to code in a intermediate level, I used codecademy to achieve this.

I chose to study in RMIT because I believe that it is one of the best universities for IT. Moreover, it is located in the wonderful city of Melbourne, which is a huge bonus. Additionally, RMIT is located in the heart of the city, I don’t have to use transport as much, because the university is located in a great place and I live 5 minutes walking from it.

During my studies, I intend to learn about the whole structure of IT, I want to be good in everything related to IT, but to be fairly honest, I would like to dedicate more effort into my programming skills, since that is something that I would like to do in the future.

Ideal Job

The role of the position is keeping “Onefootball’s web presence”, where you pick technologies and ideas and implement them to better their web presence. You will collaborate with designers and engineers, and will also have to code.

You need have several years of experience in web development, moreover you need to be able to work in groups, and more importantly, you have to know how to code in React, Vue.js, Javascript, Typescript, etc…

I don’t have experience in web development at all, but I do know how to code in javascript on a basic level.

To be able to get this position, I would need to have experience in web development, so that would be the first step, finding a job with low entry requirements for web development. From there, enhance my skills in coding, especially in those languages.
